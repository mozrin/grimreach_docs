TITLE: Grimreach Workspace – Phase 025: Deterministic Faction Pressure + Pressure-Driven Spawn Rates

INTENT
Introduce deterministic faction pressure values that influence spawn behavior.
This phase adds:
- faction pressure scores
- deterministic pressure gain/decay rules
- pressure effects on spawn rates
- world updates reflecting pressure levels
- client acknowledgement of pressure changes
- census tracking of pressure-population dynamics

No AI.  
No combat.  
No diplomacy.  
No rendering.  
Only deterministic pressure propagation.

============================================================
SECTION 1 — Define Faction Pressure Rules
============================================================

1. Introduce a minimal pressure rule structure that defines:
   - a pressure score per faction (global or per zone)
   - deterministic pressure gain (e.g., from morale or influence)
   - deterministic pressure decay (e.g., fixed decay per tick)
   - no randomness

2. Pressure rules must:
   - be simple
   - be deterministic
   - not involve combat or diplomacy

3. Pressure values must:
   - be numeric
   - be bounded (e.g., 0–100)
   - be updated every tick

============================================================
SECTION 2 — Implement Server Pressure Evaluation
============================================================

1. Integrate pressure evaluation into the server tick loop.
2. On each tick:
   - read morale values (Phase 023)
   - read influence values (Phase 021/024)
   - apply deterministic pressure gain rules
   - apply deterministic pressure decay rules
   - clamp pressure values to valid bounds

3. Pressure evaluation must:
   - not modify entity or player behavior directly
   - not trigger territory changes
   - not introduce randomness

============================================================
SECTION 3 — Apply Pressure to Spawn Rates
============================================================

1. Modify spawn rules (Phase 011) so that:
   - spawn intervals or spawn counts are multiplied by pressure-based modifiers
   - higher pressure increases spawn rates
   - lower pressure decreases spawn rates
   - all modifiers are deterministic

2. Spawn rate adjustments must:
   - be clamped to prevent runaway spawning
   - not exceed per-zone or per-type maximums
   - not introduce new entity types

3. Pressure must influence:
   - respawn cycles (Phase 013)
   - but not despawn cycles (Phase 012)

============================================================
SECTION 4 — Include Pressure Summaries in WorldState
============================================================

1. Extend the WorldState broadcast to include:
   - pressure values per faction
   - optional “boosted” or “suppressed” flags

2. Pressure summaries must:
   - remain lightweight
   - not introduce complex serialization

============================================================
SECTION 5 — grimreach_client: Acknowledge Pressure Effects
============================================================

1. When receiving a WorldState message:
   - read pressure summaries
   - detect changes in pressure
   - print simple information (e.g., “Order pressure rising: 41 → 55”)

2. Do not render pressure.
3. Do not store pressure data beyond printing summary information.

============================================================
SECTION 6 — grimreach_census: Track Pressure-Population Dynamics
============================================================

1. When receiving a WorldState message:
   - read pressure summaries
   - correlate pressure with spawn counts
   - print aggregated information (e.g., “Chaos pressure surge increasing wilderness spawns”)

2. No storage, indexing, or analytics logic is required.

============================================================
SECTION 7 — No Other Files Modified
============================================================

- Do not modify Flame components.
- Do not modify UI files.
- Do not introduce AI, combat, or rendering.
- Do not introduce diplomacy or interactions.

============================================================
COMPLETION CRITERIA
============================================================

- Server defines deterministic pressure rules.
- Pressure values update every tick.
- Spawn rates are modified by pressure.
- WorldState includes pressure summaries.
- grimreach_client prints pressure change information.
- grimreach_census prints pressure-population dynamics.
- All projects compile and run with no errors.
- No gameplay logic exists.
- No rendering or input is introduced.
- The entire workspace runs cleanly with pressure-driven spawn behavior.
